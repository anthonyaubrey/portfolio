This summer I undertook an 11 000 km drive to do backcountry camping and hiking with some of my closest friends. These images are taken with the Sony A7 and Sony 28 - 70mm lens. 

The first stop on our trip was Algonquin Provincial Park, one of my favourite places for backcountry camping and canoe trips. The last stop on our trip was Tofino, Vancouver Island.
