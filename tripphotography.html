places

A collection of images taken over the course of 3 months spent on the road. We traveled from Ottawa, ON to Vancouver Island. The journey was about 12 000 km by car and well worth it, despite many hours spent at the wheel and occasionally sleeping in Walmart parking lots. These images are taken with the Sony A7 and Sony 28 - 70mm lens. 

The first stop on our trip was Algonquin Provincial Park, one of my favourite places for backcountry camping and canoe trips. The trip consisted of visits to many National and Provincial parks. The last stop on our trip was Tofino, Vancouver Island.
